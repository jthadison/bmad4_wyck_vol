# Managing Risk Limits

Configure risk management settings to protect your capital.

## Step 1: Navigate to Risk Settings

Open Settings and go to Risk Management section.

<!-- action: Click Risk Settings -->

## Step 2: Portfolio Heat Threshold

Set maximum portfolio heat allowed.

<!-- action: Set portfolio heat to 5% -->

Portfolio heat is total risk across all open positions.

## Step 3: Campaign Risk Allocation

Set maximum risk per campaign.

Default: 2% per campaign maximum.

## Step 4: Notification Preferences

Configure alerts for risk limit breaches.

## Step 5: Review Risk Warnings

Understand what happens when limits are exceeded.

System will warn you before accepting new positions.
